Modeling the disperal of aquatic plants by float planes

In this project with the University of Alaska Anchorage, we aim to use computer vision and bayesian modeling to understand how invasive and native aquatic plants are dispesed by float planes. This anthropogenic dispersal is leading to the takeover of some Alaskan lakes by elodea and homogenisation of previously isolated ecosystems.
